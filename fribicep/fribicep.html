BICEP CURL

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Grip the barbell with underhand grip. Curl the barbell towards chest, keeping elbows stationary. Hold briefly, then lower back down. Maintain form and control.

ONE ARM DUMBBELL PREACHER CURL

Sit on a preacher curl bench with your upper arm against the pad. Hold a dumbbell in one hand, fully extend your arm, and curl the weight upward, contracting your biceps. Lower the dumbbell back down and repeat.

PREACHER CURL

Sit on a preacher curl bench, position your upper arms against the pad, and grasp a barbell or dumbbells with an underhand grip. Curl the weight upward, keeping your upper arms stationary. Lower the weight back down and repeat. Use proper form and consult a professional if needed.

HAMMER CURL

Stand with dumbbells, palms facing your body. Curl the weights upward with a neutral grip, keeping your arms close to your sides. Lower the dumbbells back down and repeat. Use controlled movements and consult a professional if needed.
