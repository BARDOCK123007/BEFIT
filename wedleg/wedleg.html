BARBELL SQUAT

Perform a barbell squat by setting up the barbell on a rack, positioning it on your upper back, bending at the knees and hips to squat down, and then pushing through your heels to return to the starting position. Keep your core braced, chest up, and maintain proper form throughout.

LEG PRESS

Sit on the leg press machine with feet on the foot platform. Lower the weight by bending your knees, then extend legs to raise the weight back up. Keep back against the backrest, avoid locking knees, and maintain control. Adjust as needed and start with lighter weights. Seek guidance if necessary.

LUNGES

Perform lunges by stepping forward, lowering your body with bent knees, and pushing through the front heel to return. Alternate legs, maintain stability, and start with lighter weights. Seek professional guidance if necessary.

LEG EXTENSION

Sit on the leg extension machine, position your feet under the padded lever, and extend your legs upward. Lower them back down and repeat. Start with lighter weights and maintain proper form.

LEG CURL

The leg curl is a hamstring exercise. Lie face down on the machine or with a resistance band around your ankles. Bend your knees, curling your heels towards your buttocks. Slowly lower your legs back down.
