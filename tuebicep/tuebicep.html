BICEP CURL

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Grip the barbell with underhand grip. Curl the barbell towards chest, keeping elbows stationary. Hold briefly, then lower back down. Maintain form and control.

CONCENTRATION CURL

Sit on a bench, feet flat, legs slightly apart. Hold a dumbbell in one hand, arm extended down against inner thigh. Lean forward slightly, keeping back straight. Curl weight up, keeping upper arm still. Contract bicep at top, then lower. Repeat and switch arms.

ONE ARM DUMBBELL CURL

Sit on the rowing machine, secure your feet, and grab Dumbbell. Pull the handles towards your midsection, squeezing your shoulder blades together. Extend your arms forward and repeat. Maintain good posture and control throughout the exercise

REVERSE GRIP BARBELL CURL

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, underhand grip on barbell. Curl barbell toward shoulders, keeping upper arms still. Contract biceps at top, then lower. Repeat.
