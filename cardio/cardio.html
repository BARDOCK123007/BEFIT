RUNNING

Running is a high-impact cardio exercise that involves moving swiftly on your feet at a faster pace. It is an effective way to improve cardiovascular endurance, burn calories, and strengthen leg muscles.

JUMPING ROPE

Jumping rope is a simple yet intense cardio exercise. It involves jumping over a rope while keeping a steady rhythm. It is a great way to improve coordination, agility, and cardiovascular fitness.

CYCLING

Cycling is a low-impact cardio exercise that can be done outdoors or on a stationary bike. It helps strengthen leg muscles, improve cardiovascular health, and burn calories. Cycling can be tailored to different intensity levels, making it suitable for people of various fitness levels.

High-intensity interval training (HIIT)

HIIT involves alternating between short bursts of intense exercise and brief recovery periods. It can include various activities like sprinting, burpees, jumping jacks, or mountain climbers.

JUMPING JACKS

Jumping jacks are a classic cardio exercise that involves jumping while simultaneously spreading your legs out to the sides and raising your arms above your head. They are an effective way to raise your heart rate,and work multiple muscle groups simultaneously.
