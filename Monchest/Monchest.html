BENCH PRESS

Lie on a flat bench with feet planted, grip the bar wider than shoulders, lower to mid-chest with elbows at 45 degrees, push back up while maintaining contact with bench and avoiding back arch or hip lifting. Increase weight gradually.

FLAT DUMBBELL FLY

Lie on a bench with dumbbells above your chest. Lower them out to the sides in an arc, keeping a slight elbow bend. Pause, then raise them back up. Focus on chest muscles and control the movement.

INCLINE BENCH PRESS

Set up an incline bench. Lie back and grab the barbell with a wide grip. Lower it to your upper chest, then push it back up. Use controlled movements and breathe properly.

DECLINE DUMBBELL PRESS

Lie on a decline bench. Hold dumbbells above your chest with palms facing forward. Lower them to the sides of your chest, then push them back up. Maintain controlled movements and proper form.

CABLE FLY

Stand in a staggered stance between cable pulleys. Grab the handles with an overhand grip. Step forward and slightly lean forward. Pull the handles together in front of you, crossing your hands. Squeeze your chest muscles, then slowly return to the starting position. Keep a controlled motion throughout.
