FRONT LAT PULLDOWN

Sit on the lat pull-down machine, grab the wide bar with an overhand grip, and pull it down in front of your body towards your upper chest. Control the movement as you slowly return to the starting position with fully extended arms. Repeat for desired reps, focusing on engaging your lat muscles. Maintain proper form and control throughout.

LAT PULL DOWN V BAR

Sit on the machine and grab the V-bar attachment. Pull the bar down towards your upper chest, squeezing your shoulder blades together. Lower the bar back up and repeat. Focus on engaging your lats and maintain proper form.

SEATED ROWING

Sit on the rowing machine, secure your feet, and grab the handles or bar. Pull the handles towards your midsection, squeezing your shoulder blades together. Extend your arms forward and repeat. Maintain good posture and control throughout the exercise. Adjust the resistance as needed.

ONE ARM DUMBBELL ROWS

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, hold a dumbbell in one hand, and hinge forward. Pull the dumbbell towards your lower chest, keeping elbow close. Squeeze back muscles at the top, then lower and repeat. Engage core and maintain form.

HYPER EXTENSION

Lie face down on a bench or stability ball. Lift your upper body using lower back muscles, keeping a straight line. Squeeze glutes and lower back, then lower down. Maintain form and increase intensity gradually.
