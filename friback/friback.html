FRONT LAT PULLDOWN

Sit on the lat pull-down machine, grab the wide bar with an overhand grip, and pull it down in front of your body towards your upper chest. Control the movement as you slowly return to the starting position with fully extended arms.

LAT PULL DOWN BACK

Sit on the machine, secure your thighs, and grip the bar with an overhand grip. Lean back slightly and pull the bar down to your chest, squeezing your shoulder blades. Return to the starting position and repeat. Use proper form and consult a professional if needed.

ONE ARM DUMBBELL ROWS

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, hold a dumbbell in one hand, and hinge forward. Pull the dumbbell towards your lower chest, keeping elbow close. Squeeze back muscles at the top, then lower and repeat. Engage core and maintain form.

BENT OVER

Bent-over exercises target the back and lower body. Examples include bent-over rows, bent-over rear delt flyes, and bent-over dumbbell reverse lunges. Use proper form and start with lighter weights, gradually increasing the load.

HYPER EXTENSION

Lie face down on a bench or stability ball. Lift your upper body using lower back muscles, keeping a straight line. Squeeze glutes and lower back, then lower down. Maintain form and increase intensity gradually.
